How We Works

Wash & Fold

A whiter-than-white shirt. A crisp cotton saree. A well-worn pair of rugged jeans. King-sized bed linen. Monday-to-Friday socks. Well, this load is going to need high levels of hygiene and cleanliness – and also a challenging amount of compact folding. We know what it means: you didn’t just download an app … you offloaded a weight off your mind.

Wash & Iron

Office wear shirt. Evening wear salwar kameez. Daily wear pyjamas. Sportswear sweat pants. Holiday wear bermudas. Hmm, that’s a time, energy and space consuming list if you ever saw one. We can confidently say this: you’re not just arranging for a laundry service now … you’re rearranging your day and life.

Steam iron

A wrinkled business shirt. A crumpled dress. A badly creased pair of trousers. A crushed uniform. A crinkled scarf. Oh-oh, if this lot is oddly familiar, strike while the iron is hot. We know what it means: you’re not looking for well-pressed garments… you’re ironing out the stresses of the day.

Dry Cleaning

A delicate silk dress. A tailored suit. A fancy evening gown. Your finest and most treasured garments need extra special care. We understand what it means: you’re not just looking for clean clothes… you’re ensuring they look their best for every occasion.
